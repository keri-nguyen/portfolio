About

During my time at UCF, I have held positions such as a Resident Assistant (RA), Marketing Assistant at the Recreation and Wellness Center, and Communications Manager for Knight-Thon. Throughout these roles, I have been able to gain leadership skills such as overseeing a team, time management, effective communication, and working with diverse leadership styles.

In my free time, I love hanging out with my cat, learning new recipes, and indulging my love for seeing new places.
