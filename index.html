About

Welcome to Petal & Bloom, your go-to boutique flower shop! We specialize in creating unique and seasonal floral arrangements for weddings, events, and everyday occasions.

Our passion for flowers and commitment to sustainability guide everything we do. We source local flowers whenever possible and aim to provide personalized service to each of our customers.

Founded by Sarah Thompson, Petal & Bloom has grown from a small shop into a beloved community favorite. Our team of skilled florists is dedicated to making your special moments even more beautiful.

Thank you for choosing Petal & Bloom! We look forward to brightening your day with our floral creations.
